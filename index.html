    Two words describe our educational vision at DPSS,Madinaguda.Think Ahead.

    What does Think Ahead mean to us? It means:

    1. Providing opportunities for students to lead at all grade levels.
    2. Embracing Experiential Learning in our pre-primary level.
    3. Knowing that the development of a child's character is as important as the development of their cognitive skills.
    4. Dedicating our primary students to the conceptual understanding so that the child is well prepared for the future.
    5. Providing a fully articulated curriculam from preschool through 6th class.

    At DPSS-, we think Ahead for the benefit of our students, every day.

    Any journey begins with just one step. But, without a clear picture of the destination, that setp is likely to be in the wrong direction. The journey to school improvement and student achievement is no different. Everyone involved administrators, teachers, parents and the students need to develop a focuses image of the goal and create a map that will lead them there together. This shared vision fosters success because of understanding and believing his/her role in the pursuits of helping them to acquire the skills they need to become productive citizens in a rapidly changing global society.

You will find that my door is always open and I am here to support you in any way possible.

Our Code of Conductarrow_upward
Our Code of Conductclose
  1. Students must be respectful to their school mates and staff members.
  2. During School hours, students are expected to communicate in English.
  3. Students must keep the campus clean.
  4. Defacing the school walls/school furniture/washroom and miscellaneous items will be treated as a misconduct and will invite action against the defaulters.
  5. Students must not indulge in any act that will tarnish the image of the school.
  6. Students must not bring cash or wear jewellery like chains, rings, bangles,anklets etc(shool is not responsible in case of loss/theft of any expensive item).
  7. Students are prohibited from bringing camers,walkmans,mobile phones,electric gadgets,magazines,pictures,tattoos,sharp instruments,blades etc, inside the school premises.
  8. In all matters, the decision of the Principal will be final and binding upon the student.
  9. Misconduct,misbehaviour and indiscipline in school transport will not be tolerated.
